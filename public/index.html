Good Questions

The honest FAQ

What is Kangen Water®? +

Kangen Water® is Enagic's brand name for alkaline, hydrogen-rich water produced by their electrolysis ionizer machines. The machines filter your tap water and then ionize it, producing several types of water (drinking, beauty, cleaning) at different pH levels. We focus on what it actually is — clean, ionized, great-tasting water — not on medical claims.

How much do the machines cost? +

Pricing varies by model and current Enagic terms, so we go over exact numbers on your free consult along with the payment options that fit you. The important part: we accept credit and debit cards, offer in-house financing, and you can pay in full or with a deposit plus installments.

Can I really try it for free? +

Yes. If you're local to us, we'll arrange free gallons of Kangen Water® so you can taste it at home for a few days. Anyone, anywhere, can also get a free info pack and a free 30-minute consultation.

What payment options are there? +

Three main ways: pay in full, pay by credit/debit card, or use Enagic's in-house financing (deposit plus monthly installments). We'll walk you through current plans and terms on your consult.

Do I become a distributor when I buy? +

With Enagic, every machine purchase also registers you as an independent distributor if you choose — meaning you can share the water and earn commissions when others buy. It's optional, and we're happy to explain how the business side works if you're interested.

Is it really a medical device? +

Yes — Enagic's ionizers are certified as medical devices by Japan's Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare, manufactured under the ISO 13485 medical-device quality standard, and used in hundreds of Japanese hospitals. It's the only water ionizer with that classification. Important: this is a Japanese regulatory certification of the device and its manufacturing — it is not a US FDA clearance, and it does not mean the water treats or cures anything. We don't make health claims.

Does Kangen Water cure or treat health conditions? +

No — and we won't tell you it does. Even though the machine is a certified medical device in Japan, that certification is about the device itself, not a promise about your health. The machines are not approved to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and we make no medical claims. We talk about hydration, taste, and water quality. For any health condition, please talk to your doctor.
